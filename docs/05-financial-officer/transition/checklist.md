# Transition Checklists

---

## Outgoing FO — Tasks Before Leaving Office

Complete these before or by August 31 (the date the incoming FO takes office).

### Transitional Report (40% of transition honorarium)
- [ ] Write a comprehensive transitional report covering:
  - All committees you sit on: work done in the past year, current agenda
    items, direction for the coming year
  - Student staff positions associated with the FO role: current roles and
    suggestions for the coming year
  - Review of important media/issues from the past year
  - Successes and failures of initiatives; suggestions for improvement
  - Upcoming deadlines and a timeline of key events
- [ ] Confirm the report is a new document (not a copy of a prior year's report)
- [ ] Submit report to EOC and incoming FO before August 31

### Training of Incoming FO (15%)
- [ ] Introduce incoming FO to key staff: GM, Accountant, Bookkeeper
- [ ] Walk through day-to-day tasks: credit card approvals, cheque requisitions,
      Zoho Expenses workflow, monthly report review
- [ ] Attend at least 3 HFC meetings jointly with incoming FO
- [ ] Arrange instruction by the Financial Controller (or Accountant/Bookkeeper)
      on cheque signing procedures and reviewing supporting documentation

### Consultation (15%)
- [ ] Remain available for at least 1 month after August 31 to answer questions
- [ ] Inform incoming FO of any outstanding issues to be resolved

### Organization of Files (15%)
- [ ] Organize all Dropbox folders: label, clean up, archive completed items
- [ ] Ensure all working documents are in Dropbox (not only on local drives)
- [ ] Remove all personal belongings from office
- [ ] Return GSS equipment (debit card, office keys, etc.) to GM
- [ ] Ensure all system access is transferred (see [System Access](accounts-access/))
- [ ] Clean and organize physical office space per EOC direction

### Position Reviews (15%)
- [ ] Participate in reviews for any position that the incoming FO requests

## Incoming FO — First 30 Days

### Before Taking Office (Two Weeks Prior to August 31)
- [ ] Attend HFC meeting(s) with outgoing FO
- [ ] Begin receiving transition honorarium (Bylaw 8.6.2)
- [ ] Review outgoing FO's transitional report
- [ ] Meet with GM for office orientation
- [ ] Shadow outgoing FO on: credit card approval cycle, Zoho Expenses,
      banking systems, cheque signing

### Week 1 (August 31 – September 7)
- [ ] Confirm access to all systems (Zoho Expenses, Zoho Books, Scotia Online,
      Scotia Connect, Plooto, Payworks, Dropbox, Notion)
- [ ] Receive FO debit card from bank (coordinate with GM)
- [ ] Confirm signing authority has been updated at the bank (two of three:
      President / FO / VP UAA)
- [ ] Obtain investment account signatory update paperwork from GM
- [ ] Review the approved annual budget for the current fiscal year
- [ ] Attend first HFC meeting as FO

### Weeks 2–4
- [ ] Perform credit card expense approvals for the prior month
- [ ] Review the monthly financial report with the Accountant and GM
- [ ] Present first financial update to Council
- [ ] Read GSS Bylaws §8.5.6, Executive Policy §3.5, HF Policy §3.9 and
      Appendices I and II
- [ ] Read and understand the Statement of Investment Policy
- [ ] Confirm Plooto/payment platform access with GM
- [ ] Confirm Payworks access and understand payroll approval cycle
- [ ] Confirm Vancity migration status with GM
- [ ] Confirm investment manager transition (RBC Dominion Securities) status
      with GM
