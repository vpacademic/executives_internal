# Banking, Payments, and Payroll

---

## GSS Bank Accounts

The GSS maintains five accounts:

| # | Account | Purpose | Policy reference |
|---|---------|---------|-----------------|
| 1 | **General Operating** | Day-to-day operations | — |
| 2 | **GSFA / Housing Aid** | Financial aid to UBC graduate students in unforeseen hardship; not accessible to current GSS executives or GSS graduate student employees | Policy 15 |
| 3 | **Event Fund** | Financial rebates for events/projects organized by Affiliate Organizations (AOs) | Policy 9 |
| 4 | **CPIF** (Capital Projects Improvement Fund) | Improve spaces under GSS control; purchase equipment/furniture with lifespan ≥ 3 years | Policy 16 |
| 5 | **Investment** | GICs and Mutual Funds — funds not required in the short term | See [Investment Accounts](investment/) |

## Debit and Credit Cards

**FO debit card:** Grants authority to withdraw and transfer funds on behalf
of the GSS. Subject to variation based on the bank or credit union.

**President debit card:** Information and verification purposes only. Does not
grant withdrawal/transfer authority.

**Corporate credit cards** ($10,000 limit each):
- General Manager
- Events Manager
- Administrative Assistant

Credit card top-ups are subject to budget approval by the FO. Once the FO
approves, the GM tops up the card.

## Banking Software

### Scotia Online
- **Purpose:** Paying bills, checking balances, sending Interac e-transfers,
  accessing the Scotiabank account.
- **Who has access:** FO, President, GM.

### Scotia Connect
- **Purpose:** Transferring and depositing funds, checking balances, accessing
  payroll solutions, paying bills, sending electronic funds transfers (EFT).
- **Who has access:** Bookkeeper, Accountant, GM, FO.

### Vancity Credit Union

{: .note }
As of the guide's last update, the GSS is in the process of migrating partial
operating cash to Vancity Credit Union as part of a divestment and climate
action initiative. Once the financial transaction and system seem suitable for
continued use, the GSS plans to move all operating accounts to Vancity.
Verify current status with the GM before acting on any Vancity account.

## Plooto (Payment Platform)

Plooto is the GSS's online payment software platform. After expenses are
approved in Zoho Expenses, the GM executes e-transfers through Plooto. The
Bookkeeper enters payment notifications and confirmations into the accounting
system in the first week of the subsequent month.

{: .note }
As of the guide's last update, Plooto is subject to change in 2024–25.
Verify the current payment platform with the GM.

## Payworks (Payroll and Timesheets)

**Purpose:** Timesheet tracking and payroll processing.

**Payroll schedule:** Semi-monthly — 24 payrolls per year.
- Pay period 1st–15th: pay date is the **22nd** of the same month.
- Pay period 16th–31st: pay date is the **7th** of the next month.
- If a pay date falls on a weekend or holiday, payment is made ahead of
  schedule. Exception: the last payroll of the calendar year (Dec 16–31) has
  a pay date of Dec 31; no payment on Jan 7.

**Workflow:**
1. Bickert Management (Bookkeeper) sends GSS Executives and staff a task
   requesting payroll updates via Zoho Projects.
2. Timesheets are uploaded to Payworks semi-monthly.
3. GSS approves Pay Run by 2:30 PM.
4. Bickert Management finalizes the Pay Run; payroll is released.

**Employee types:**
- **Salary employees:** GSS executives, full-time staff, Health and Dental
  Coordinator. Paid annual salary; not eligible for overtime unless specifically
  approved by the GM and HR Committee.
- **Hourly employees:** Part-time staff, all Work Learn student positions.

**FO role:** New hires and terminations must be signed off by the President
and FO.
