## Executive Event Participation

Executives can attend most GSS events and programs at no cost. Exceptions may
apply for high-cost events or events with limited space and high demand.

Executive presence is valuable because students often share feedback more
comfortably in event settings than in formal meetings.

## Event Support Requests

Use the staff-maintained Events Form for events requiring GSS staff support.
Because the form is an internal SharePoint document, do not publish the link on
the public guide. When circulating the form internally, include all relevant
parties in the email so each role can see where their involvement is needed.

Build in reasonable deadlines, especially during busy periods. Submit requests
at least 1 week in advance; 1 month or more is preferred when staff support,
advertising, or event planning is needed.

## Advertising and Newsletter Timing

Plan around the Wednesday newsletter deadline. Requests made with fewer than 2
weeks' notice, measured from that deadline, will likely have weaker attendance
because there is less time to advertise the event.
