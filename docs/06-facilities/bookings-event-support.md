# Bookings and Event Support

Use this page as the public operating reference for booking GSS spaces and
requesting GSS staff support for events.

## Venue Booking Workflow

| Step | Guidance |
|------|----------|
| Submit the request | Use the public [venue rental page](https://gss.ubc.ca/venue-rental/) or email the Events Manager if staff have directed you to do so |
| Lead time | Submit at least 5 business days in advance; 1 month or more is preferred |
| Date priority | Bookings are first come, first served, so request preferred dates as early as possible |
| Names | Use the current venue names: Thea's Lounge, Leon's Lounge, Graduate Life Centre, Study Room, GSS Loft, and Ballroom |
| Promotion | Encourage departments and graduate student groups to consider GSS spaces when they need bookable venues |

## Space Reference

| Space | Capacity / use | Key features |
|-------|----------------|--------------|
| Thea's Lounge | 120 theatre-style; 150 standing reception | A/V equipment, furniture, bar counter, bar fridge |
| Leon's Lounge | 40 theatre-style; 15-20 classroom-style | A/V equipment and furniture |
| Graduate Life Centre | Graduate student social, study, and relaxation space | Open to graduate students during regular hours; event use during regular hours must be free to all graduate students |
| Study Room | Small internal meetings and private Teams meetings | Bookable internally by GSS staff/executives and CUPE 2278 |
| GSS Loft | Graduate student events in the AMS Nest | Self-serve setup/takedown; follows AMS booking policies |

## Alcohol and Special Event Permits

Alcohol is not permitted without a Special Event Permit and a server who has
Serving It Right or Special Event Server certification. The event client is
responsible for applying for the permit and arranging the certified server.

Apply at least 4 weeks in advance when possible. A minimum of 2 weeks is
required.

## Fees and Supervision

Graduate student events, defined as events where more than 50% of attendees are
graduate students, have no charge for the room itself. Other costs may still
apply, including labour, supervision, and linen rentals.

Executives can act as supervisors for GSS spaces when they are in the building
for the entire duration of the booking. Confirm expectations with the Events
Manager before relying on executive supervision for a booking.

## Building and Loft Access

For Thea Koerner House access outside regular hours, contact
`events@gss.ubc.ca` and provide the student ID information requested by staff.
Do not publish key-box locations, door codes, alarm details, or other
access-sensitive instructions in this guide.

The GSS Loft is used by AMS from May through August. Office keys can be
requested after AMS summer control ends; for 2026 requests, do not assume keys
can be issued before August 15, 2026. The board room and Loft offices are for
GSS staff and executives only, and office spaces may need to be shared.
