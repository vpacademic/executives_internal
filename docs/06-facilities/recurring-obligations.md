# Recurring Obligations

This page tracks facilities obligations that should be reviewed on a regular
cycle.

| Cadence | Obligation | Responsible role | Source status |
|---------|------------|------------------|---------------|
| Monthly | Review active facilities issues and project blockers | To be confirmed | Needs current operating source |
| Quarterly | Review agreement deadlines, notice periods, and renewals | To be confirmed | Needs final agreement links |
| Annually | Confirm space inventory, access list, and asset records | To be confirmed | Needs current staff procedure |
| As needed | Escalate repairs, safety concerns, or agreement disputes | To be confirmed | Needs current escalation route |

## Review Checklist

- Confirm active project statuses and next decision points.
- Check whether any agreement notice or renewal date is approaching.
- Confirm maintenance issues have an owner and follow-up date.
- Verify facilities costs are routed through the correct finance workflow.
- Update transition notes for any unresolved facilities issue.

{: .note }
Add exact dates only after they are verified against final agreements or current
staff-maintained records.
