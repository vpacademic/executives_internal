# Agreements and Leases

This page indexes final facilities agreements and leases relevant to executives
and staff.

{: .warning }
Publish only signed/final agreement links. Do not link negotiation folders,
drafts, redlines, or folders containing sensitive private correspondence.

| Agreement | Final link | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| Office lease | Link pending | Add final/executed agreement link when provided |
| Ballroom sublease or current CUS arrangement | Link pending | Add final/executed agreement link when provided |
| Head lease | Link pending | Add final/executed agreement link when provided |

## Link Collection Order

1. Office lease.
2. Ballroom sublease or current CUS arrangement.
3. Head lease.

## Agreement Entry Standard

Each agreement entry should include:

- Final agreement link.
- Parties to the agreement.
- Effective date and expiry or renewal date.
- Notice periods.
- Responsible GSS role for monitoring the agreement.
- Any Council, HFC, or Executive Committee reporting dependency.
