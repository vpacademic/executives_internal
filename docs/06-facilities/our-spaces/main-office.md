# GSS Main Office (Thea Koerner House, Rm 452)

The GSS Main Office is the core workspace for executives and staff.

## Operating Reference

| Topic | Current guide status |
|-------|---------------------|
| Access and keys | Coordinate through the Events Manager or General Manager; do not publish exact key-handling details |
| Office equipment | Needs current inventory or asset list |
| Records kept on site | Needs verification before publication |
| Mail and deliveries | Needs current procedure from staff |
| Maintenance requests | Needs current contact and escalation route |

## Building Access

Thea Koerner House regular access hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to
5:00 p.m. Executives who need building access outside those hours should contact
`events@gss.ubc.ca` and provide the student ID information requested by staff.

Shared keys must be returned before leaving the GSS Office so that staff and
executives can continue to access the office and related spaces. The public
guide should not list the exact key-box location or access mechanics.
