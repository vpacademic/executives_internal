# Other Rooms and Shared Spaces

This page summarizes shared rooms and facilities that GSS executives or staff
use, coordinate, or monitor.

## Space Inventory

| Space | Use | Booking notes |
|-------|-----|---------------|
| Thea's Lounge | Larger receptions, presentations, and theatre-style events | 120 theatre-style, 150 standing reception; includes A/V equipment, furniture, bar counter, and bar fridge |
| Leon's Lounge | Smaller presentations or classroom-style meetings | 40 theatre-style, 15-20 classroom-style; includes A/V equipment and furniture |
| Graduate Life Centre | Graduate student social, study, and relaxation space | Open Monday to Thursday, 9:00 a.m.-9:00 p.m., and Friday, 9:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m., except holidays; bookable only on weekends and holidays |
| Study Room | Private Teams meetings and small in-person meetings | Bookable internally by GSS staff/executives and CUPE 2278 |
| GSS Loft | Graduate student events and social/study use when not booked | Bookable only for graduate student events; follows AMS booking policies and is generally self-serve |
| Storage areas | Operational storage | Access and inventory need private verification before publication |

## Graduate Life Centre Use

The Graduate Life Centre is intended as a home away from home for graduate
students. Events held in the GLC during regular operating hours must be free to
all graduate students.

## GSS Loft Use

The GSS Loft is in the AMS Nest. AMS uses the Loft from May through August.
When the Loft is available for GSS use, it is normally a self-serve venue:
clients set up and take down the room themselves. The board room and offices are
for GSS staff and executives only.
