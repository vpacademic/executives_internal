# Key Contacts

This page maps facilities responsibilities by role. Add names and direct
contact details only if they are approved for publication in this guide.

| Role / body | Facilities relationship | Notes to confirm |
|-------------|-------------------------|------------------|
| General Manager | Day-to-day facilities operations and escalation | Confirm current scope |
| President | Executive oversight and major agreement coordination | Confirm current role |
| Financial Officer | Budget, payments, and finance approvals connected to facilities | Confirm finance workflow |
| Facilities or building contact | Maintenance, access, building coordination | Add only approved contact route |
| CUS or agreement counterparty | Ballroom or shared-space arrangement, if current | Confirm final agreement and contact route |

{: .warning }
Do not publish personal phone numbers, private emails, emergency access details,
or vendor account information unless the GSS has approved that information for
this site.
